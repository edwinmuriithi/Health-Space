ABOUT US

We develop and distribute inexpensive, easy to use and interconnected healthcare systems that run on the web and mobile devices to clinics, hospitals, laboratories, radiographers and pharmacies that then enable them enhance service delivery to their clients and improve on their record keeping while at the same time collecting this data electronically to centralized Collabmed servers. Our vision is to become the leading healthcare information manager in Africa and be among the top place Afya Pap uses Al and behavioural science to help our users change their habits, reduce the financial burden of managing their conditions, and improve their health.
